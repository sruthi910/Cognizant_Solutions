Community Bulletin

The Greenfield community is organizing a weekend cleanup event across local parks and playgrounds. Volunteers of all ages are welcome to participate. The event will begin at 9 AM and continue till noon, followed by refreshments and a brief appreciation ceremony.

Local businesses have also pledged support by providing supplies and sponsoring snacks. This initiative, led by the town’s Youth Council, aims to promote civic engagement and environmental awareness. Residents are encouraged to bring reusable gloves and water bottles.

In other news, the monthly Farmers’ Market will now be held every first and third Saturday at the central plaza. Vendors are invited to register early, and the public can expect fresh produce, artisan goods, and live music.
